# Complex Analysis, Visually

I'm Max — an incoming math major at UCSB's College of Creative Studies. This site is where I'm working through complex analysis on my own, pairing the standard theorems and proofs with interactive visualizations that make the geometry behind them tangible. Everything here is built as I learn it, in order, starting from the big picture and working toward the results that make the subject feel almost unfairly powerful.

Each lesson pairs a written explanation with a visualization you can actually play with — drag points, adjust sliders, and watch the theorems become visible rather than just symbolic.
