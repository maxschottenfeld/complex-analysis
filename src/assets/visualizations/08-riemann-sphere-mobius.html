Möbius Maps on the Riemann Sphere

Lesson 8 · a square grid patch rides on the sphere: move the sphere and the patch's projected shadow deforms under the induced Möbius map, over a fixed reference grid — rigid rotations realize exactly the isometric maps (PSU(2)); scaling and translation are non-rigid flows across the same fixed sphere. Inspiration drawn from Douglas Arnold & Jonathan Rogness's video Möbius Transformations Revealed.
